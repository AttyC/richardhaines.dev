RedwoodJS is a new opinionated full stack, serverless web app framework that takes all the good parts of what a JAMstack website offers and packages it up into something that promises to offer all the good and much more. Its still in alpha phase but i decided to take it for a spin to learn a little about how it works and how its workflow compares to my favorite dev tool Gatsby.
